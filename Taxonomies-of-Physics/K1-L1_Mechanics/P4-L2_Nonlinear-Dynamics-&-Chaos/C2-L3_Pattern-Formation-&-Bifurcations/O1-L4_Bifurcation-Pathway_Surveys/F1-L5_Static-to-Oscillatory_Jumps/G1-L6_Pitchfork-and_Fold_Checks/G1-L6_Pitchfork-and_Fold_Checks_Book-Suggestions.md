# G1 Pitchfork and Fold Checks — Book Suggestions

These titles support diagnosing pitchfork and fold bifurcations in experiments and models.

## Bifurcation Theory References
- *Elements of Applied Bifurcation Theory* — Yuri Kuznetsov. Comprehensive treatment of normal forms and stability analysis.
- *Nonlinear Dynamics and Chaos* — Steven Strogatz. Offers intuitive explanations of saddle-node and pitchfork bifurcations.

## Laboratory and Modeling Guides
- *Experiments in Nonlinear Physics* — Harry Swinney. Describes physical setups exhibiting pitchfork and fold behavior.
- *Pattern Formation and Dynamics in Nonequilibrium Systems* — Cross & Hohenberg. Links bifurcations to pattern onset in fluids and chemistry.

## Computational Tools
- *AUTO Tutorial for Bifurcation Analysis* — Doedel et al. Provides software instructions for tracing bifurcation diagrams.
